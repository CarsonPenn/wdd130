How it all began...

This all began nearly a decade ago, not on a bloody battlefield, castle, or a writers office. It began on a walk home from church amongst friends.

Four young boys talking about adventure and daring escapades lead them to tell stories of what they might do if they were big and strong.

Now almost a decade since those Sunday afternoons; we've decided to write down and share our childhood dreams and fantasies.
