Three Species one world

In mercy the Dragons created man. In continued mercy they created the Gorilla People and the Dragon People. They share one world and one purpose. To serve the Great Dragons and obtain the principles of purity and righteousness.

Sharing a world Three times the size of our own, they create vast civilizations and achieve wonderful feats of architecture and become individual societies of justice and truth.

Mankind

Created first by the great dragons, they are the most spread out and largest of the three populations. They have no limit to the amount of rings they may have. The first humans were Alam and Ava. There are many dissenters amongst the humans who follow the Dark Dragon.

Gorilla People

The second species to be created. Two children of Alam and Ava were cursed by one of the Titans, the Titan Gorath. Cursing them to be gorillas he hoped to have them as slaves. The two cursed children found favor in the sight of the Water Dragon Gali and turned the curse into a blessing. They are limited to 10 rings they may hold and are incredibly strong.

Dragon People

Created last by the Great Dragons. When Ava gave birth to a set of twins they were still born. The Great Dragons looked upon them with mercy and healed them using their pure DNA. The two children were genetically modified and their lives were saved. They have the ability of flight and are limited to one ring bonded to their body at birth.
