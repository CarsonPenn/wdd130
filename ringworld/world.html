Three Species One World

The Dragons of Creation formed the world and the peoples thereon. First the Humans, then turning a curse to a blessing, the Gorilla People, healing a plague they created the last species, the Dragon People. They share one world and one purpose. To serve the Great Dragons and obtain the principles of purity and righteousness.

Sharing a world like that of our own, they create vast civilizations and achieve wonderful feats of architecture, becoming individual societies of justice and truth.

The Humans

Created first by the great dragons, they are the most spread out and largest of the three populations. They have no limit to the amount of rings they may have. The first humans were Alam and Ava. There are many dissenters amongst the humans who follow the Dark Dragon. Humankind is more prone to war against their own species. There are either times of great peace or war unrest.

Gorilla People

The second species to be created. Two children of Alam and Ava were cursed by one of the Titans, the Titan Gorath. Cursing them to be gorillas he hoped to have them as slaves/worshippers. The two cursed children found favor in the sight of the Dragons. They created a pool of pure water for them to wash in. Turning the curse into a blessing. They are limited to 10 rings they may hold and are incredibly strong. They have facial features similar to gorillas, they are exceptionally strong and have varying colors of fur.

Dragon People

Created last by the Great Dragons. When Ava gave birth to a set of twins they were still-born. The Great Dragons looked down upon them with mercy and healed them using their own pure DNA. The two children were genetically modified and their lives were saved. They have the ability of flight and are limited to one ring bonded to their body at birth. They have bright scales and dazziling designs.
