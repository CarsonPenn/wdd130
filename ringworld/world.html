Three Species One World

In mercy the Dragons created man. In continued mercy they created the Gorilla People and the Dragon People. They share one world and one purpose. To serve the Great Dragons and obtain the principles of purity and righteousness.

Sharing a world Three times the size of our own, they create vast civilizations and achieve wonderful feats of architecture and become individual societies of justice and truth.
